About

The Gen Z star Kid Laroi is now on tour! After years of being a feature on many tours, you can now see him perform as the main event. Read more to learn about the event!

Have you always wanted exlcusive merch from the performers? This is your chance, now exclusive only available during the tour. What are you waiting for? Get your tickets before they are sold out!
